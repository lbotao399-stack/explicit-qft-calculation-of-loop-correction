# N=4 ordinary-space: Q_1 current relevant branches

## 1. Working split

$$
J^\mu_{Q_1}
=
J^\mu_{\psi\text{-branch}}
+
J^\mu_{F\text{-branch}}
+
(\text{other branches, ignored for no-derivative }2\to1).
$$

## 2. \(\psi\)-output branch

用于
$$
X_iX_j\to \Psi^k.
$$

仅保留 schematic form
$$
J^\mu_{\psi\text{-branch}}\sim (D X)\,\Psi.
$$

对应 triangle family
$$
\partial_\mu J^\mu_{\psi\text{-branch}}
\quad+\quad
V_{A X X}.
$$

## 3. \(F\)-output branch

用于
$$
X_i\Psi^j\to F,
\qquad
\Psi^jX_i\to F.
$$

仅保留 schematic form
$$
J^\mu_{F\text{-branch}}\sim F\,\bar\Lambda,
\qquad
\bar\Lambda:=\bar\lambda_{4\dot +}.
$$

对应 triangle family
$$
\partial_\mu J^\mu_{F\text{-branch}}
\quad+\quad
V_{X\Psi\bar\Lambda}.
$$

## 4. Relevant cubic vertices

$$
V_{A X X}
\qquad
\text{for}\qquad
A,X,X,
$$

$$
V_{X\Psi\bar\Lambda}
\qquad
\text{for}\qquad
X,\Psi,\bar\Lambda.
$$
